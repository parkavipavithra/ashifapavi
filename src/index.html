HENNA FASHION

My goal is to first learn how to prepare the Henna paste by going through all the steps and of course the simplest steps for learners to easily learn and remember and also find other alternatives for
Henna paste preparations and provide information about the different products and where to get them. Secondly I want to learn how to make the
Henna cone the easiest way possible and also give other ways of acquiring the cone. Finally I want to learn and make Henna tattoo sketches.
I am going to learn both the Arabic and Indian henna designs which are not too complicated and combine all my work to make a booklet for beginners.
So in general I learn how to make a Henna applicator from recycled material, learn how to mix and apply Henna and in the end I want to be able to apply
henna on hands and submit that as my final product.
